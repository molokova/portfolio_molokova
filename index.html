Case Study 1

The "Product" Turnaround

Project: Higher Ed Product Relaunch (Skillfactory)

Challenge

A high-stakes program suffered from low engagement (NESSA 53.1) and high operational costs.

Solution

  • Audit & Pivot: Identified disconnect between theory and practice.
  • Action: Shifted to Project-Based Learning (PBL). Redesigned webinars from "lectures" into interactive workshops.

Key Results

Learning Satisfaction +40.7% (53.1 to 74.7)
Operational Expenses -45% (Optimized)
Profitability 98.8% (from 91.4%)
